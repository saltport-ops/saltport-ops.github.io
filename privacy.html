i.Introduction

SaltPort Group LLC ("SaltPort," "we," "us," or "our") is a Maine limited liability company that operates short-term and long-term rental properties in coastal and urban Maine, including Laughton House & Cottages in New Harbor and a residential building on Grant Street in Portland.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you stay at one of our properties, provide services to us as a vendor or contractor, visit our website, or otherwise interact with us.

By making a reservation, providing services, or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

ii.Information We Collect

Information from Guests

When you book a stay at one of our properties — whether through Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com, or directly with us — we may collect:

  • Name, email address, telephone number, and mailing address
  • Reservation details, including dates, party size, and special requests
  • Payment information (processed by booking platforms or payment processors; we do not store full payment card numbers)
  • Government-issued identification, if required by law or property policy
  • Communications you exchange with us before, during, and after your stay
  • Information related to your stay, including check-in and check-out details and any incidents or damages

Information from Vendors and Contractors

When you provide services to us as a vendor, contractor, or service provider, we may collect:

  • Name, business name, email address, telephone number, and mailing address
  • Tax identification numbers and other documentation required for payment and tax compliance
  • Service-related information, including scheduling, work performed, and invoicing
  • Communications related to the services you provide

Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect limited technical information, including IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referral source. Our website is a simple static site and does not currently use tracking cookies, analytics, or third-party advertising technologies.

Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third parties, including:

  • Booking platforms (Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com) regarding reservations
  • Payment processors regarding transactions
  • Property management software providers
  • Background check or identity verification services, if required

iii.How We Use Information

We use personal information for the following purposes:

  • Managing reservations and stays: processing bookings, providing check-in instructions, and coordinating logistics
  • Guest communications: responding to inquiries, providing operational support, and following up after stays
  • Vendor and contractor coordination: scheduling services, processing payments, and maintaining business records
  • Property operations: maintaining and improving our properties, managing access, and ensuring guest safety
  • Payment processing: facilitating transactions via third-party payment processors
  • Legal compliance: meeting tax, accounting, regulatory, and lodging-tax obligations
  • Safety and security: preventing fraud, protecting our properties, and addressing damage or incidents
  • Service improvement: analyzing operational performance and improving our offerings

iv.How We Share Information

We share personal information only with parties necessary to operate our business, and only as described below:

Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers that perform services on our behalf, including:

  • Booking platforms (Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com) for reservations originating on those platforms
  • Property management software providers (such as Hostaway) for operational coordination
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • SMS communication providers (such as Twilio) for operational text messages
  • Artificial intelligence service providers used for guest communications and operational support, subject to data protection terms
  • Cloud storage and software providers used in the ordinary course of business
  • Accountants, bookkeepers, and tax professionals
  • Legal counsel, when required

Vendors and Contractors

To coordinate property services, we may share limited reservation or scheduling information with vendors and contractors, such as cleaners, maintenance providers, and service personnel.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose personal information when required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

Business Transfers

If SaltPort Group LLC or any of its assets is involved in a merger, acquisition, sale of assets, or other business transaction, personal information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We Do Not Sell Personal Information We do not sell, rent, or lease personal information to third parties for monetary or other valuable consideration. We do not sell, rent, or share mobile telephone numbers or text-messaging consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

v.SMS Communications

SaltPort sends operational SMS messages to guests, vendors, and contractors to support our property operations. This section provides specific information about our SMS practices.

Types of Messages

Operational messages may include:

  • Booking confirmations and reservation reminders
  • Check-in and check-out instructions, door codes, and Wi-Fi credentials
  • Scheduling and coordination of property services
  • Updates regarding maintenance, weather, or operational issues
  • Responses to guest or vendor inquiries

How You Are Enrolled

You are enrolled in our SMS communications through one of the following:

  • As a guest: by providing your mobile telephone number during the reservation process on a third-party booking platform (such as Airbnb, Vrbo, or Booking.com), or directly to SaltPort
  • As a vendor or contractor: by providing your mobile telephone number when engaged to provide services to SaltPort

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on operational needs. Guests typically receive a small number of messages related to a specific stay; vendors and contractors receive messages as needed for service coordination.

Costs

Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply. SaltPort does not charge you for receiving SMS messages.

Opting Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation message, after which no further messages will be sent. You may also opt back in by replying START.

Help

For support related to SMS communications, reply HELP to any message, or contact us using the information in the Contact Us section below.

Mobile Information Privacy We do not share or sell mobile telephone numbers or SMS opt-in information with any third party or affiliate for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing is limited to subcontractors and service providers that support our SMS communications (such as our messaging platform provider) and is used solely to operate the messaging service.

vi.Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy or as required by law. Specifically:

  • Reservation and booking records: retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to comply with tax, accounting, and lodging-tax requirements
  • Vendor and contractor records: retained for a minimum of seven (7) years to comply with tax and business-record requirements
  • Communications: retained for the duration of the business relationship and a reasonable period thereafter
  • SMS opt-in and opt-out records: retained to comply with applicable telecommunications regulations

When personal information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

vii.Your Rights and Choices

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal retention requirements
  • Portability: request that we provide your information in a portable format
  • Opt-out of SMS: reply STOP to any SMS message
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in the Contact Us section. We will respond within the time required by applicable law.

viii.California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA") and California Privacy Rights Act ("CPRA") provide you with additional rights, including:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to delete personal information, subject to certain exceptions
  • The right to correct inaccurate personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to limit the use of sensitive personal information
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information below.

ix.International Visitors

SaltPort is based in Maine, United States, and our services are intended for guests within the United States. If you access our services from outside the United States, please be aware that personal information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data-protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

If you are located in the European Union, United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with comprehensive data-protection laws, additional rights may apply under those laws, including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

x.Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of eighteen (18), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child without verified parental consent, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us.

xi.Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is one hundred percent secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

If we become aware of a data-security incident affecting your personal information, we will notify you and applicable regulators as required by law.

xii.Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, applicable laws, or our services. When we make material changes, we will update the "Effective Date" and "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
